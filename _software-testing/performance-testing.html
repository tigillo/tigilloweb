Performance Testing

With our efficient performance testing services, check your products' speed, stability, and responsiveness under different workloads.

Performance testing is essential to ascertain if your product is performing at ease without any bottlenecks. A highly performing application and software facilitates a smooth and positive user experience and ultimately higher user engagement with your brand.

Is your application or software performing as expected?

We at Tigillo ensure that your product is tested under different workloads to identify performance metrics such as throughput, memory, responsiveness, bandwidth, CPU interrupts per second, etc.

Our performance testing services, led by expert software testers, identify any parameter that impedes the performance of the application or software. Once we find out the issues, we will report it's impact to business-critical features and also help you to sort the performance issues out for you right away.

Load Testing

We perform load testing prior to deployment using the most advanced testing practices and tools to determine if your website or application performs well even under high load volume.

Endurance Tests

We test your product using the best tools and techniques and identify how well it endures stress factors until it crashes.

Test In Prod-like Environment

We run the the performance tests on a prod-like environment to not to disturb your clients.

Autoscaling

We test your website and application for future workloads and anticipated stressors with our load and stability testing services, helping you set up auto-scaling in the cloud environments.

Continuous Delivery

Tigillo's performance testing integrates performance testing into the software development life cycle, ensuring that bottlenecks are identified at each sprint before deployment.

Cost Optimization

Identifying key bottlenecks at the early stage can significantly reduce cost and optimize performance after your application or website is deployed.
